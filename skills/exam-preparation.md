## Capability

Produces targeted revision materials including summaries, flashcards, and practice questions tailored to the specific exam format and syllabus.

## When to Use

- Active revision phase before exams
- Creating practice materials for specific topics
- Generating mock exam papers

## Inputs

Syllabus content, exam format, topic areas to focus on, difficulty level

## Outputs

Revision summaries, flashcard sets, practice questions with model answers, and exam technique tips
